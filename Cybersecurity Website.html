Cybersecurity Insights

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information, extort money, or disrupt normal operations.

Famous Hacks and Hackers:

  • Kevin Mitnick: Known as one of the most famous hackers, Mitnick hacked into dozens of systems, including the Pentagon and large corporations, before being caught.
  • Anonymous: A decentralized group of hackers known for cyber-attacks on governments, institutions, and corporations.
  • Albert Gonzalez: Responsible for one of the largest credit card thefts in history, stealing millions of credit card numbers.
  • Stuxnet: A cyber weapon developed to attack Iran's nuclear facilities, showcasing how malware can impact physical systems.

Always remember: the best defense is awareness and proactive protection of your data and systems.
